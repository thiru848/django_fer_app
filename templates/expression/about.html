"Knowledge opens up different dimension of opportunities"



The website mainly focuses on providing the better exprience for users to know about them better.
It is a on going development of tool, trying to make better predictions through lots of various cases.
The main feature of the product is identify the gender & emotions based Facial Expression Recognition,
then chat bot interaction & music recommendation based on the mood.
This website provides an interface to view and download report generated after the session &
doctor consultation which is best designed to the
model used in our website. We are open to the suggestions that the users request on us.

The User classes that we have are Patient and Doctor.
Anyone who created account as patient interact with the system through chat bot & sessions.
Through those monitoring we are able to identify the behaviour of person based on some given scenario
which will be added based on the data generated from all the modules. Report generated is directed to doctor
to give some ideas or any solution required by user.
Users can also manage their profile information if they find it to be false after signup.
